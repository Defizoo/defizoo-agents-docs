## Things to Watch For

- **Pixel accuracy:** Are the dimensions exactly right for the platform?
- **Brand consistency:** Does he use the correct colors/style for each protocol?
- **Rendering quality:** Do the output images look professional and clean?
- **Template reuse:** Can he create templates that work for recurring content?
- **Iteration speed:** Does he adjust quickly based on feedback?
- **Creative range:** Can he do both data-driven graphics and creative visuals?
