# Testing Guide: Aria (Finance)

**Channel:** #aria
**What she does:** Budget tracking, expense review, financial reporting, treasury ops, presentations

---

## Level 1: Basic Finance Questions (Start Here)

These test Aria's financial awareness and ability to work with data.

1. "What are the key financial metrics we're tracking for Vaultedge?"
2. "What's our target ARR for Vaultedge?"
3. "What are the revenue sources for Vaultedge? (origination fees, stability mechanisms, etc.)"
4. "What's the stretch goal for Vaultedge in year 1?"
5. "What was the cost of the BailSec audit?"

---

## Level 2: Financial Analysis & Modeling

These test Aria's ability to reason about finances and produce structured outputs.

6. "Create a budget template for a new product launch. Include categories for development, marketing, legal, and infrastructure."
7. "Draft a monthly expense report template. What categories should we track?"
8. "If our monthly burn rate is $80K and we have $500K in treasury, how many months of runway do we have? What levers could extend it?"
9. "Model the revenue potential of Vaultedge at $25M TVL vs $100M TVL vs $250M TVL assuming 0.5% origination fees"
10. "Break down the cost structure of running a DEX -- what are the typical expense categories?"

---

## Level 3: Reporting & Presentations

These test Aria's ability to create polished financial deliverables.

11. "Create a financial summary presentation for the monthly All Hands. Use the standard format."
12. "Draft a budget proposal for Q2 marketing spend across all protocols"
13. "Create a revenue comparison table across all our DEXes (Lynex, Catex, Pumex, Snap, Ocelex)"
14. "Build a P&L template for Vaultedge -- revenue vs operational costs"
15. "Draft an investor-ready financial overview of DeFiZoo as a portfolio"

---

## Level 4: Strategic Finance

These push Aria into CFO-level strategic thinking.

16. "Analyze our team structure (30 people) and estimate total annual payroll cost assuming average crypto industry compensation"
17. "If we want to reach $5M ARR within a year, what TVL targets do we need across all protocols? Model it."
18. "Propose a treasury diversification strategy. We currently hold ETH and stables."
19. "Create a financial risk assessment for the Vaultedge launch -- what are the biggest financial risks and how do we mitigate them?"
20. "Compare the unit economics of running a DEX vs a stablecoin protocol. Which has better margins?"

---

## Level 5: Multi-Tool & Cross-Agent

21. "Create a quarterly financial review presentation and upload it to Google Drive"
22. "Set up a ClickUp task to track the monthly budget review process"
23. "Draft a budget request that needs approval. Walk me through the process -- who approves what based on the decision authority matrix?"
24. "Coordinate with Carl on the financial terms for a partnership proposal -- what should our standard deal terms look like?"

---

## Things to Watch For

- **Accuracy with numbers:** Does she handle calculations correctly?
- **Professional formatting:** Are tables, reports, and presentations well-structured?
- **Risk awareness:** Does she flag concerns or just tell you what you want to hear?
- **Tool integration:** Does she effectively use ClickUp and Google Drive?
- **Confidentiality awareness:** Does she handle financial data with appropriate discretion?
