# Testing Guide: Carl (Sales & BD)

**Channel:** #carl
**What he does:** Partnerships, business development, proposals, pitch decks, market research

---

## Level 1: Research & Intelligence (Start Here)

These test Carl's ability to research markets and gather business intelligence.

1. "Who are the top 5 stablecoin protocols by TVL right now?"
2. "Research which DeFi protocols on Base have stablecoin integrations"
3. "What's the competitive landscape for decentralized stablecoins? Who are the main players?"
4. "Find 3 potential partnership targets for USDVE integration"
5. "What grant programs are available on Base right now? Are any relevant to Vaultedge?"

---

## Level 2: Proposals & Outreach

These test Carl's ability to create professional BD materials.

6. "Draft a partnership proposal for integrating USDVE as collateral on Aave"
7. "Write a cold outreach email to a DEX asking them to add USDVE/USDC as a trading pair"
8. "Create a one-pager about Vaultedge for potential partners"
9. "Draft a liquidity provider outreach message -- target: institutional DeFi funds"
10. "Write a follow-up message for a partnership lead that's gone cold"

---

## Level 3: Pitch Decks & Presentations

These test Carl's presentation creation skill (PowerPoint).

11. "Create a pitch deck for Vaultedge targeting DeFi-native investors. 10 slides max."
12. "Build a partnership deck showing DeFiZoo's full product portfolio"
13. "Create a sales presentation for onboarding a new DEX client"
14. "Design a 5-slide overview of the USDVE opportunity for a potential integration partner"
15. "Make a competitive analysis presentation: Vaultedge vs top 3 competitors"

---

## Level 4: Strategic BD

These push Carl into higher-level strategic thinking.

16. "Map out the ideal integration partners for USDVE in tiers: Tier 1 (must-have), Tier 2 (nice-to-have), Tier 3 (long-term). Explain your reasoning."
17. "Propose a BD strategy for the first 90 days after Vaultedge launch. What partnerships should we prioritize and why?"
18. "Analyze which blockchain ecosystems beyond Base would be the best expansion targets for Vaultedge. Rank them."
19. "If we wanted USDVE to become the default stablecoin on Linea, what's the playbook?"
20. "Create a partner scoring framework -- what criteria should we use to evaluate potential partnerships?"

---

## Level 5: Complex Scenarios

22. "We got a warm intro to the Chainlink team. Draft a message proposing a deeper collaboration around oracle feeds for Vaultedge."
23. "A competitor just launched on Base. Analyze their approach and suggest how we should position against them."
24. "Draft a term sheet template for revenue-sharing partnerships with other protocols"
25. "Create a full BD pipeline tracker template with stages: Lead, Contacted, Negotiating, Committed, Live"

## Cross-Agent Tests

## Things to Watch For

- **Research depth:** Does he find real, current data when browsing the web?
- **Professional tone:** Are proposals polished and investor-ready?
- **Strategic thinking:** Does he go beyond surface-level and identify non-obvious opportunities?
- **Presentation quality:** Are the PowerPoint decks well-structured with clear narratives?
- **DeFi knowledge:** Does he understand protocol mechanics well enough to pitch them convincingly?
