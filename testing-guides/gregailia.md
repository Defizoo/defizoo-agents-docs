These test GregAIlia's ability to think ahead, synthesize, and produce useful outputs.

## Level 4: Multi-Step Workflows

30. "Check all upcoming birthdays for the next 30 days, draft personalized messages for each person, and suggest which ones might want a special celebration based on their seniority and role."
31. "Create a complete onboarding packet: welcome message, first-week schedule, key contacts list, and a 30-day goals template for a new QA engineer."

## Things to Watch For

- **Accuracy:** Does she get birthdays, roles, and policies exactly right?
- **Tone:** Is she warm and people-focused, not robotic?
- **Memory:** If you correct something, does she remember it in the next message?
- **Boundaries:** She should know what she knows and admit what she doesn't. She shouldn't make up team members or policies.
