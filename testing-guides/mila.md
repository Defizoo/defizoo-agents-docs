## Things to Watch For

- **Brand consistency:** Does she differentiate between Vaultedge, Lynex, Catex, etc.?
- **Crypto-native voice:** Does the content sound like it's from DeFi insiders, not a generic marketing agency?
- **Research quality:** When she browses the web, does she return useful, current data?
- **Iteration:** When you say "make it shorter" or "more degen", does she adjust well?
- **Tool use:** Does she actually create files and upload to Drive when asked?
